About

I'm an AI-systems researcher. My work sits where rigorous machine-learning methods meet real scientific and clinical problems — a PhD in ICT (Big Data Analytics) layered on top of an MSc in Biomedical Engineering.

I've built deep-learning architectures (GNNs, Transformers, Multiple-Instance Learning, Mixture-of-Experts), scaled analytics over Spark/Hive/Hadoop, and taken models from messy signals and images to interpretable, leakage-free predictions. Today I'm a Research Engineer at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano's Cybersecurity Lab, building AI models for network-flow anomaly and intrusion detection — after leading AI-driven radiomics for metastatic prostate cancer on the AIRFRAME project.

The through-line across medicine, neuroscience, and large-scale data is the same: design the right learning system for the structure of the problem, then make its answers trustworthy.

Medical AI Current

AIRFRAME — Prostate-cancer therapy-response prediction

Predicting Responder vs. Non-Responder in metastatic prostate cancer from lesion-level PSMA-PET radiomics, accounting for intra-patient tumor heterogeneity. Three complementary architectures: a hybrid Attention-MIL model fusing radiomics + clinical data, a Mixture-of-Experts MIL that discovers lesion phenotypes via clustering, and patient-graph GNNs that model relationships between lesions.

  • Attention-MIL
  • Mixture-of-Experts
  • GNN (PyG)
  • PyRadiomics
  • Patient-level CV

Neuroscience In progress

EEG suspense decoding — Vision Transformer pipeline

Classifying pre- vs. post-suspense brain states from movie EEG. A Vision Transformer (vit_base_patch16_224, adapted to 5-channel connectivity images) fine-tuned against a rigorous classical-ML baseline — a StandardScaler → SelectKBest → PCA pipeline with GridSearchCV over SVM, RF, XGBoost, KNN, and MLP using StratifiedGroupKFold to prevent subject leakage.

  • Vision Transformer
  • timm / PyTorch
  • EEG connectivity
  • GridSearchCV
Neuroscience MSc thesis

EEG depression quantification — a continuous Beck-aligned index

An objective depression index from 19-channel EEG across 75 patients: RQA and nonlinear features, t-SNE reduction, a fuzzy classifier with Dempster–Shafer channel fusion, and MARS regression onto the Beck Depression Inventory — reaching 0.92 Pearson correlation (5% PRD) with the clinical index.

  • Fuzzy / NN
  • RQA features
  • t-SNE
  • Dempster–Shafer
Big Data ICEIS 2024

ClassCube — classifying big OLAP data cubes

A framework that classifies high-dimensional OLAP cubes after PCA / dimension-selection into a reduced cuboid lattice. The headline result: 4D reduced models match ~6D accuracy at a fraction of the compute — mitigating the curse of dimensionality and enabling cloud-scale, near-real-time analytics.

  • OLAP
  • PCA
  • Cuboid lattice
  • SVM / LogReg
Big Data IEEE CBDCom 2022

Bayesian-network transportation analytics

Predicting bus-arrival delays from open GTFS transit data with Bayesian networks over (stop, route, arrival-time) triplets. Different network topologies (serial, diamond) capture conditional dependencies, with a ~93.7% best-configuration occurrence at a ~1% relative-error target.

  • Bayesian networks
  • GTFS
  • Probabilistic inference
  • Open data
